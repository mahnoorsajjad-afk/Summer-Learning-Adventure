📅 6-Week Plan

Weekly Summer Challenges

Tick off each activity as you go! When all tasks in a week are ticked, a ✉️ Send to Teacher button appears. 🎉

⏳ Challenges unlock on 20 June 2026!

Get ready — the Summer Learning Adventure starts soon! 🌟

📚

Week 1 · From 20 Jun

Reading Explorer

  • Daily Reading: Read a storybook for 15 minutes every day.
  • Word Power: Find and write down 5 brand-new words you learned this week.
  • Art Studio: Draw your favourite character from a story you read.
✅ Submitted to Teacher!
🔢

Week 2 · From 27 Jun

Maths Detective

  • Shape Hunter: Find 3 dimensional shapes around your room!
  • Market Maths: Count small change or add numbers on a grocery receipt with a parent.
  • Pattern Power: Spot or draw repeating patterns around your home.
✅ Submitted to Teacher!
🌿

Week 3 · From 4 Jul

Science Explorer

  • Nature Watch: Observe a local tree for 10 minutes. Draw its leaves!
  • Weather Log: Track the weather for 5 days — sunny, windy, or cloudy?
  • Kitchen Science: Drop objects in water with a parent. Sink or float?
✅ Submitted to Teacher!
🛠️

Week 4 · From 11 Jul

Young Inventor

  • Recycle Challenge: Collect clean cardboard, bottle caps, and boxes. Build a model fort or robot!
  • Show & Tell: Explain to a family member how your invention works.
✅ Submitted to Teacher!
👾

Week 5 · From 18 Jul

Coding Fun

  • Logic Step: Complete a beginner puzzle using direction arrows on a screen.
  • Dance Party: Use forward, backward, left, and right moves to make a character dance!
✅ Submitted to Teacher!
🤖

Week 6 · From 25 Jul

AI & Robotics Explorer

  • Robot Chat: Discuss with your parents how robots help humans — farming, cars, and vacuum cleaners!
  • Smart Tools: Explore a safe image-recognition game with a parent to see how computers "see".
✅ Submitted to Teacher!
