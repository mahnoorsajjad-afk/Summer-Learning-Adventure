💌 Dear Parents & Guardians,

Summer 2026 · KG Learning Adventure

We hope this message finds you and your family in wonderful health and high spirits! As the summer holiday begins, we wanted to share something very special with you — our KG Summer Learning Adventure website, designed with love for your little ones. 🌟

This website is your child's very own summer learning space. It is packed with fun, play-based activities that gently keep their minds active and curious throughout the holiday — all from the comfort of your home. There is no pressure and no homework feel — just joyful learning!

📚 What's Inside the Website?

🗓️6 Weekly Challenges — Each week has 3 fun tasks covering phonics, numbers, art, nature, music, and more. Your child ticks off each task as they complete it!
🎲Summer Bingo — A fun bingo game with activity squares your child can complete and mark off. Three in a row earns a BINGO!
🌐Learning Resources — Carefully selected free websites, videos, and platforms such as Phonics Bloom, Starfall, and more — all safe and age-appropriate for KG learners.
🎵Jolly Phonics Song — A video song to help your child practise all letter sounds in a playful and memorable way.
🏆Achievement Certificate — Once your child completes the challenges, they can generate and print their very own personalised Summer Star certificate!
🎮Family Fun Activities — Story Time, Number Fun, Art & Colour, Nature Walk, Sing & Move, and Shapes — six interactive mini-activity sets you can enjoy together as a family.

✅ How Does It Work?

  1. Open the website on any phone, tablet, or computer.
  2. Click on KG from the home screen.
  3. Each week, help your child complete the 3 activities listed under My Challenges.
  4. Once all 3 tasks are ticked, the ✉️ Send to Teacher button appears — click it to submit via Google Form.
  5. Explore the Resources, Bingo, and Family Activities sections anytime!

💡 Tips for Parents

⏰There is no deadline pressure — complete each week at your own pace.
🤝Sit with your child and do the activities together — it makes it even more fun!
📸Take photos of your child's work — we would love to see them at the start of the new school year!
🌟Celebrate every little achievement — your encouragement means the world to them.

We are truly grateful for your continued support and partnership. Your involvement at home makes such a beautiful difference to your child's learning journey. We cannot wait to hear all about their summer adventures when we see them in September!

Wishing you and your family a safe, happy, and wonderful summer holiday. 🌈☀️

With warm regards,

💡 Quick Tips for Summer Learning

  • 📖Read together daily — even 10 minutes of shared reading builds vocabulary and a love of books.
  • 🔢Count everything — steps, apples, buttons, spoons. Maths is everywhere at home!
  • 🎨Let them create — drawing, painting, and building with blocks all develop fine motor skills and creativity.
  • 🌿Go outside — nature walks, bug hunts, and planting seeds teach science through real experience.
  • 💬Ask open questions — "What do you think will happen?" and "Why?" build critical thinking.
  • ⏱️Keep it short and fun — 15–20 minutes of focused activity is perfect for KG children.

Parent Information Letter

Summer Learning Adventure 2026 · Grades 1 & 2

Date: June 2026

Dear Parent / Guardian,

We are pleased to present the Summer Learning Adventure 2026, a structured six-week digital learning programme designed specifically for Grade 1 and Grade 2 students at ABQ Sohar International School. This initiative has been carefully developed in alignment with the Cambridge KS1 curriculum to ensure that students maintain academic momentum throughout the summer holiday period.

📋 Programme Overview

The programme runs from 20 June to 1 August 2026 and is organised into six themed weeks. Each week introduces age-appropriate activities spanning Reading, Mathematics, Science, Coding, Artificial Intelligence, and Online Safety. The website features seven interactive sections designed to keep learning fun and engaging throughout the holidays.

🖥️ Website Sections

  • 🏠
    Home
    The main landing page with topic activity cards, quick navigation shortcuts, and the full Phonics Programme embedded below.
  • 🔤
    Phonics Programme Home Page
    A six-week interactive phonics programme embedded on the Home page. Each week focuses on a different phonics concept — Short Vowels, Consonant Blends, Magic-e, Digraphs, Vowel Teams, and High-Frequency Sight Words. Each week includes three exercises: Word Sort, Fill-in-the-Blank, and Multiple Choice — all with instant feedback and scoring.
  • 🗓️
    Weekly Challenges
    Six themed challenge cards — one per week. Children tick off tasks as they complete them. Once all tasks are done, a Send to Teacher button appears.
  • 🌐
    Learning Resources
    Curated free platforms for English, Maths, Science, Coding, AI for Kids, and Parents — including recommended tools such as Reading Eggs and Maths Seeds.
  • 🎲
    Bingo Challenges
    Interactive offline bingo boards for Reading, Science, and Maths — children tap squares as they complete activities and aim for four in a row.
  • 🔢
    Interactive Maths Activities New
    Four tabbed activity sets covering Number Recognition (matching, counting dots, bigger/smaller), Addition (MCQ, typed answers, story sums), Number Order (before & after, sequencing, odd/even), and Missing Numbers (gap-fill equations and counting patterns).
  • 📝
    My Learning Log
    A weekly activity log where children record what they did in Reading, Maths, Science, and Coding. Completed logs are submitted to the teacher via Google Form.
  • 🏆
    Certificate of Achievement
    Upon completing the programme, children enter their name and receive a personalised Summer Learning Explorer Award, which can be printed or downloaded.

✅ What is Expected of Your Child

  • 📅
    Complete one weekly challenge set per week
    Each week contains 2–3 short tasks. Your child should tick each task on the website as it is completed.
  • 📝
    Record activities in the Learning Log
    After completing the week's tasks, your child should fill in the My Log section on the website with a brief note about what they did.
  • 📤
    Submit to the teacher each week
    Once the log is complete, your child should click the Send to Teacher button. The Google Form will open and your child should fill it in carefully before clicking Submit.
  • 🎮
    Explore the interactive activities
    The website includes quizzes, games, and phonics exercises. Encourage your child to attempt at least one activity per topic each week.
  • 🏆
    Earn the Summer Explorer Certificate
    Upon completing the programme, your child may enter their name on the Certificate page and download or print it as a record of achievement.

👨‍👩‍👧 Your Role as a Parent or Guardian

  • 🖥️
    Provide supervised access to the website
    Please ensure your child accesses the programme through a device with a stable internet connection. The website is accessible at: sciencewithmissmahnoor.vercel.app
  • ⏰
    Encourage a consistent routine
    We recommend setting aside approximately 20–30 minutes per day, three to four days per week, for your child to engage with the programme.
  • ✍️
    Enter your name in the Parent Name field
    When your child submits their weekly log, please ensure your name is entered in the Parent Name field as confirmation of your involvement and awareness.
  • 🛡️
    Supervise internet use at all times
    All external platform links included in the Resources section are safe, free, and education-focused. However, we strongly advise that parents remain present during any online activity.

📅 Weekly Schedule at a Glance

  • Week 1 📚 Reading Explorer 20 – 26 June
  • Week 2 🔢 Maths Detective 27 June – 3 July
  • Week 3 🌿 Science Explorer 4 – 10 July
  • Week 4 🛠️ Young Inventor 11 – 17 July
  • Week 5 👾 Coding Fun 18 – 24 July
  • Week 6 🤖 AI & Robotics Explorer 25 July – 1 August

📌 Important Note Regarding Submissions

All weekly submissions are recorded directly in the teacher's Google Form. When your child clicks Send to Teacher, the Google Form will open and must be filled in completely each week. Submissions are reviewed by the class teacher and contribute to the student's summer engagement record.

💡 Recommended External Platforms & Built-in Activities

We particularly recommend Reading Eggs for literacy development and Maths Seeds for numeracy practice. In addition, the website now includes a dedicated Interactive Maths Activities page with number recognition, addition, number ordering, and missing number exercises — all designed for KS1 learners and accessible directly on the site without any external login.

We greatly appreciate your continued support in fostering a love of learning during the summer holiday period. Should you require any further clarification, please feel free to reach out to your child's class teacher.
